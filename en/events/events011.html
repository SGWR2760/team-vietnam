Welcome to 11th Vietnam and Japan greeting event! We would like to hold our first greeting event of the year.

Whether you've participated before or this is your first time, let's have some fun together! The theme this time is "puzzle solving"! Actually, we did this theme in the second round!

This is a game in which you solve riddles and codes by using your "inspiration". In our social events, Vietnamese and Japanese people work together to think and plan the events 🌍, so even those who are not confident in their Japanese skills can participate 👍 Please bring your friends! Participation fee is one thousand yen per person!

I look forward to meeting you! 🌸🌸
