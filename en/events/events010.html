10th Annual Vietnam🇻🇳✖️Japan🇯🇵 Exchange party❗️

Event ｜ October 25, 2020
日本語のページを表示 Hiển thị trang tiếng việt

This is the tenth time we've held this exchange event in Vietnam.

The theme is "Bun Cha Festival". We'll be cooking Buncha (pictured on the cover), a dish that even former President Obama liked to eat, with Vietnamese residents in Nagoya. We were planning to have a picnic two years ago and last time, but it got rained out 💦. Now, let's upgrade and enjoy a picnic outdoors while cooking and eating delicious Vietnamese food with Vietnamese people!

Whether you are a newcomer❗️or a regular visitor❗️you're welcome!

This party is organized by Japanese and Vietnamese people together. Even if you don't speak Japanese yourself, or don't know much about Vietnam, it's okay 👍. Feel free to join us 🎶.

Pray for sunny skies that day ☀️. Maybe I should make a teru teru bozu...? 😆
